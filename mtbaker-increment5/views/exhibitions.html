Exhibitions

Digital Dreams

Digital Dreams explores how artificial intelligence and machine learning collaborate with artists to create evolving digital artworks. Visitors can observe how algorithms generate visual patterns, music, and immersive projections in real time.

Interactive Realities

Interactive Realities invites visitors to step into installations that respond to movement, sound, and touch. Using motion tracking and projection mapping, the exhibition transforms visitors from observers into participants.
